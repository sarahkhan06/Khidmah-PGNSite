About PGN

Not a Fraternity.
Something More.

Phi Gamma Nu (PGN) at UT Dallas is a co-ed professional development organization — we operate with the brotherhood and structure of a fraternal organization, but our mission is singular: to develop the next generation of professionals, leaders, and change-makers.

We are not Greek Life. We are something different — a brotherhood built on accountability, mentorship, and professional excellence. Whether you're pre-med, studying business, majoring in ATEC, or anything in between, PGN UTD is where careers begin and lifelong bonds are formed.

Our chapter at UTD is part of the national Phi Gamma Nu network, and we maintain the highest standards for brotherhood, service, and professional development — because we believe that your network is your net worth.
